This gives importance to scheduling of pods, it allows high priority workloads to be given preferences.
In fact if an high priority workload cannot be scheduled, the scheduler tries to terminate the pods with lower
priority to make space for the high priority workload.

The kubernetes components gets the highest priority, which has a value of 2,000,000,000. You can only assign values
to your workload between the range -2,147,483,648 to 1,000,000,000. The higher the number the better the priority.

kubectl get priorityclass
