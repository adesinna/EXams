There are two ways data can be, either at rest like in hard disk or in transit like https request.

If you store data without encrypting the data either at rest or in transit and you lose it, the data is not secured.

Asymmetric encryption, is used where you encrypt with your public key and decrypt with  your private key.

Cloud KMS:
Cloud Key Management Service, it allows you to allow you to create keys to encrypt and decrypt.

i. Google Management Key, here google manage your keys and no configuration is needed
ii. Customer Management Key, here you manage your own key in Cloud KMS
iii. Customer supplied key, here you supply your own key to Cloud KMS.
